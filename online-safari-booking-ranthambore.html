Ranthambore National Park in India offers two safari timings:
Morning Safari: Usually starts around 6:30 AM and ends at 10:30 AM.
Afternoon Safari: Typically starts around 2:30 PM and ends at 6:30 PM.

Please note that these timings may vary slightly depending on the season and local regulations, so it's a good idea to check with the park authorities or your tour operator for the exact timings during your visit.
